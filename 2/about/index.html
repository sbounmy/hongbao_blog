Why Paper in 2025 ?

We believe that paper is the simplest form of storage for Bitcoin. It's also the most secure way to store Bitcoin, even if you have a hardware wallet you still need to backup your 24 words seed phrase.

And it's the only way to give Bitcoin without knowing the recipient address.

Finally, it's always a good idea to bring a physical gift for a birthday or Christmas, rather than arriving empty-handed.

Why AI ?

We built it for our friends and family. We started designing custom paper wallets with the faces. We were not sure if it would be useful for other people.

Then ChatGPT Image Generation was released. We started to use it to generate the paper wallets.
